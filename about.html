About

My name is Vlad Kolmorgan and I am an aspiring programmer. Even though I've learned some programming already that doesn't mean the learning ever stops, not in a year not in 10. Programmers are always learning new things in order to become better at what they do. I've always had a passion for gaming, so finding out I myself could make them it quickly became my childhood dream. Now that dream is becoming reality, and soon enough I won't just be playing games I love, I'll be making them too.

  • Learn how to code my very own games
